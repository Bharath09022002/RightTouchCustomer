GDPR Compliance

Right Touch Mobile App

If you are located in the European Union (EU), your personal data is processed in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

We process personal data only where we have a lawful basis, including:

  • To provide and operate our services
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • For legitimate interests such as improving services, security and fraud prevention
  • With your consent, where required (e.g., location access)

Your GDPR Rights

EU users have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data (“Right to be Forgotten”)
  • Restrict or object to certain processing activities
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact: support@righttouch.in. Your data may be processed and stored on secure servers outside the EEA with appropriate safeguards in place. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
